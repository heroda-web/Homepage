Dyslexia Book

Home Work Play About

For this project, I chose to focus on dyslexia and created a 500-page book set exploring the topic through both research and personal experience. As someone with dyslexia, I wanted to share what it was like growing up with it, and how it shaped the way I think, learn, and create. I designed two companion books (Book A and Book B) which together form the full 500 pages. Each book was hand-stitched using a traditional kettle stitch binding. This project was deeply personal and, in many ways, therapeutic, giving me the space to reflect on a lifelong challenge and turn it into something meaningful and expressive.
