In this project, we worked in teams to design a magazine centered on a local Richmond community. Our group focused on Carytown, with each team member responsible for a different section of the publication. My section, Art in Carytown, explored the neighborhood’s creative culture through tattoo studios, street art, stationery shops, and galleries. I designed five editorial spreads and one title page, emphasizing visual storytelling and cohesive layout design
