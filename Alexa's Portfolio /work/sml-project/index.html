SML Project

Home Work Play About

Small:

A series of postcards inspired by songs by The Decemberists . I selected specific tracks and translated their narratives into letterforms, creating visual “letters” shaped by sound and story.

Medium:

A marine radar mapping landmarks from my own life. A heart sits at the center, with each landmark positioned closer or farther away depending on its emotional significance to me.

Large:

A sandbox installation filled with bottles containing letters from friends and family. Each person was given a prompt and asked to write a letter in response. I coffee-dyed the paper, sealed the letters in bottles, and embedded them in the sand.
